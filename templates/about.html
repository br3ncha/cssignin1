About the Project

The target audience for our app includes CSSIers and their RAs. Our app will give RAs a better way to keep track of CSSIers, whether it be on the weekends or weekday mornings. It will give CSSIers a way to let their RAs and everyone else aware of their whereabouts. Contact information of all the CSSIers, as well as RAs and leaders, are available on our app to help everyone stay connected. You are also able to view the CSSI calendar to keep updated on what's happening everyday!
